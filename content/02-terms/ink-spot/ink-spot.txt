Title: Ink Spot

----

Text:

A [Usenet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Usenet) newsreader [application](/terms/application) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [DejaVu Software](/sources/dejavu). Ink Spot was also available for the Windows CE platform.
