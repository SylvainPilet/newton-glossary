Title: Font

----

Sort: Font

----

Text:

A complete set of characters or glyphs from a given typeface design. For example, [System](/terms/system-font) is one of the typefaces included with the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), but 12pt System Bold is one of the fonts.
