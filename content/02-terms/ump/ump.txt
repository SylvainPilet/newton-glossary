Title: UMP

----

Text:

Upgraded MessagePad. Generally referring to a [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) device that has had the system memory upgraded to the equivalent of a [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100). Also referred to as [UMP2K[(/terms/ump2k).
