Title: Post MessagePad Syndrome

----

Text:

Originally [coined by Woo Lee](http://marc.info/?l=newtontalk&m=99875436316308&w=2), this condition commonly afflicts people who have given up their Newton device for another handheld platform. Also known as [PMS](/terms/pms).
