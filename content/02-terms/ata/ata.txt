Text:

Advanced Technology Attachment

An interface standard generally used for data storage devices. For example, [Paul Guyot](/sources/paul-guyot) developed a [device driver](/terms/device-driver) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) which facilitates the use of non-linear ATA [stores](/terms/store) such as [compact flash](/terms/compactflash) cards.
