Title: PKG

----

Text:

An extension used to identify Newton [package files](/terms/package-file). Confusingly, a number of other [operating systems](/terms/operating-system) including Solaris, BeOS, Mac OS X, and iOS also use the `.pkg` extension to denote installable software packages.
