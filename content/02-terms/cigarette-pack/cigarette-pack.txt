Title: Cigarette Pack

----

Text:

A nickname for the original Newton fax modem. So called because its dimensions were similar to that of a package of cigarettes. Also known as the [Cigarette Case](/terms/cigarette-case) or [Cigarette Box](/terms/cigarette-box) modem.
