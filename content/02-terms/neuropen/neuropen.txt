Title: Neuropen

----

Text:

The official product name for the software-based [print recognizer](print-recognizer) system developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/). More commonly known as [Rosetta](/terms/rosetta) and [Mondello](/terms/mondello), the Neuropen moniker was never used publicly.

<h2>Additional Information</h2>

[Larry Yaeger: Handwriting Recognition That Actually Works](http://wwnc.newtontalk.net/resources/WWNC2006_Yaeger_HWR.pdf) (PDF)
