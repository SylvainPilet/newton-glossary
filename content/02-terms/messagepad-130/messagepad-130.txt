Title: MessagePad 130

----

Text:

The first Newton device to incorporate backlighting. The MessagePad 130 shares the same case styling as the [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110) and [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120). Commonly referred to as an [MP130](/terms/mp130).
