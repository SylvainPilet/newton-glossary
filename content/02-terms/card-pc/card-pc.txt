Title: Card (PC)

----

Text:

A small, removable peripheral card primarily used for memory expansion and application storage. Other peripherals based on the [PC card](/terms/pc-card) form factor are networking cards, modems, hard disk drives, and [video adapters](/terms/ez-presenter).
