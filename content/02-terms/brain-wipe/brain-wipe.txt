Title: Brain Wipe

----

Text:

The colloquial term for performing a [hard reset](/terms/hard-reset) on a Newton device, effectively erasing all user data and system preferences.

_Further reading:_
[Newton MessagePad: Different Types of Reset](https://support.apple.com/kb/TA33869)
