Title: Green Widow

----

Text:

A somewhat negative name given to the partner, spouse, or significant other of a Newton user, particularly a Newton user suffering from the [Green Bug](/terms/green-bug). Refers to the inexplicable amount of time some Newton users spend with their devices, rather than their partners.
