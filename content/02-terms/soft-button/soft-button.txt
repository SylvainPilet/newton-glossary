Title: Soft Button

----

Text:

A user modifiable [button](/terms/button) located in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) on [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices. Soft buttons may be customized by dragging [application](/terms/application) and package [icons](/terms/icon) from the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer).
