Title: PocketWeb

----

Text:

The first web browser available for use on a handheld device. PocketWeb was specifically designed for the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) platform by the [PDA Developers Group](http://www.teco.edu/pda/) at [TECO](http://www.teco.edu/) in 1994.
