Title: IrDA

----

Text:

Infrared Data Association

An industry body developing standards for [infrared](/terms/infrared) data transfer. Also the [infrared](/terms/infrared) communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) standard used in later [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) devices.
