Title: Fitaly

----

Date: 21 November 2017

----

Text:

An alternative onscreen keyboard for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [Textware Solutions](/sources/textware). The Fitaly keyboard is ergonomically designed to minimize the amount of [pen](/terms/pen) travel between keys, based on how frequently [characters](/terms/character) are used in the English language.

Versions of the Fitaly keyboard were also available for Windows and Palm platforms.
