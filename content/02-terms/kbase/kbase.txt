Title: Kbase

----

Text:

A nickname for the AppleCare Knowledge Base or [AppleCare Knowledge Archive](/sources/applecare-knowledge-archive), a technical support database consisting of thousands of documents related to [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) products and services. Each document in the library could be referenced by a unique identification number. The Knowledge Archive was preceded by the Apple Tech Info Library ([TIL](/terms/til)) and superseded by the current [Apple Support](http://www.apple.com/support/) site.
