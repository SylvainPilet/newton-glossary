Title: Endpoint

----

Text:

A type of communications connection such as a serial connection, modem, [infrared](/terms/infrared) beam, or [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) network. Also an [object](/terms/object) created from `protoBasicEndpoint`, or one of its derivative [protos](/terms/proto), that controls a real-time communication session. This object encapsulates and maintains the details of the specific connection, and allows you to control the underlying communication tool.
