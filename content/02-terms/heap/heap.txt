Title: Heap

----

Text:

The area of [system memory](/terms/system-memory) reserved for use by program functions and executable code. On a Newton device, heap often refers specifically to the [NewtonScript heap](/terms/newtonscript-heap) and [C++ heap](/terms/c-heap). Heap is also referred to as [frames heap](/terms/frames-heap).
