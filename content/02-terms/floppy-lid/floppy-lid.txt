Title: Floppy Lid

----

Text:

The result of the failure of the tension spring found in the hinge of an [eMate](/terms/emate-300) lid. The tension spring will normally hold the lid and [display](/terms/display) at any given angle. When the tension spring fails, the lid flops down.
