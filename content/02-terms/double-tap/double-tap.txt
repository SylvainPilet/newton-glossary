Title: Double Tap

----

Sort: Double Tap

----

Text:

To touch or [tap](/terms/tap) the same spot on the screen, or nearly the same spot, twice in rapid succession with the pen. Essentially the same type of action as double clicking a mouse button on a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer) or equivalent device.
