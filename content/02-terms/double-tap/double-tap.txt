Title: Double Tap

----

Text:

To touch or [tap](/terms/tap) the same spot on the screen, or nearly the same spot, twice in rapid succession with the [pen](/terms/pen). Essentially the same action as double clicking a mouse button on a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer), laptop, or equivalent device.
