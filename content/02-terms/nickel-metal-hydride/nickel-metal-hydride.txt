Title: Nickel Metal Hydride

----

Text:

A rechargeable [battery](/terms/battery) technology that is not susceptible to [memory effect](/terms/memory-effect), unlike batteries based on [Nickel Cadmium](/terms/nickel-cadmium) technology. Also referred to as a [NiHM](/terms/nimh) battery.
