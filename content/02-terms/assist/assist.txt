Title: Assist

----

Text:

A function built into Newton device that can automatically perform certain tasks, such as dialing a phone number, searching for information, or scheduling an appointment. Assist is accessed by entering a [request word](/terms/request-word) and corresponding [action](/terms/action) into the [assist drawer](/terms/assist-drawer). Also referred to as [assistant](/terms/assistant) and [Newton assistant](/terms/newton-assistant) on early version of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).
