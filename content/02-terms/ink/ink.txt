Title: Ink

----

Text:

The raw data for input drawn by the user with the [pen](/terms/pen). Also referred to as [raw ink](/terms/raw-ink) or [sketch ink](/terms/sketch-ink).

Ink is also the name of the Mac OS X preference pane used to control the transplanted Newton [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system, originally dubbed [Inkwell](/terms/inkwell).
