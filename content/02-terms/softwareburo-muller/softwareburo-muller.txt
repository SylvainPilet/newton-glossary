Title: Softwarebüro Müller

----

Text:

A German software development company that produced numerous [applications](/terms/application) and utilities for the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) and Windows CE platforms. Their most notable product was the eponymous [SBM Utilities](/terms/sbm-utilities).
