Title: Calligrapher

----

Sort: Calligrapher

----

Text:

The cursive [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system found in all Newton devices except the [eMate](/terms/emate). Calligrapher was also used as the [print recognizer](/terms/print-recognizer) in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 1.x devices such as the [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100).
