Title: Newtsbug

----

Text:

A low level [debugger](/terms/debugger) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) development that runs on a Macintosh connected to a Newton device via serial cable. It is used to help debug C, C++, or [ARM](/terms/arm) Assembler code running on the Newton device. The name is a nod to the [MacsBug](https://web.archive.org/web/20070520203932/http://developer.apple.com/tools/debuggers/MacsBug) debugger for Mac OS.
