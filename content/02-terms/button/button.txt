Title: Button

----

Text:

A small [graphical user interface](/terms/graphical-user-interface) object that performs an action when tapped. Also, a physical button that performs a hardware action on the device. For example, pressing the the [reset button](/terms/reset-button) on a Newton device performs a [soft reset](/terms/soft-reset).
