Title: Craft Digital Assistant

----

Text:

A specialized [personal digital assistant](/terms/personal-digital-assistant) (PDA) that can access the Craft Access System, allowing telecommunication field technicians to access work orders and other information through the telephone system. Also known as a [CDA](/terms/cda), playing off the term [PDA](/terms/pda). The Harris Dracon [SuperTech 2000](/terms/supertech-2000) is an example of a craft digital assistant running [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).
