Title: Siemens Online Terminal

----

Text:

A Newton device purported to have been in development by [Siemens](https://www.siemens.com/). The prototype device was to be an “all-in-one communication terminal” employing a [user interface](/terms/user-interface) based on [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) and combining several communication services including wireless telephony, fax, voice messaging, web access, and email.

A third party device that utilizes a licensed version of the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) is often referred to as a [clone](/terms/clone). Siemens also developed two versions of the [NotePhone](/terms/notephone), a Newton OS device incorporating a telephone.
