Title: Dock

----

Date:

----

Text:

The act of connecting a Newton device to another computer, in most cases to transfer data. Dock is also one of the built-in [applications](/terms/application) in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.1, specifically used to perform the action of connecting to another computer.
