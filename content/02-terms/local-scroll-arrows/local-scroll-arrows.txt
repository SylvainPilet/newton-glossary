Title: Local Scroll Arrows

----

Sort: Local Scroll Arrows

----

Text:

A pair of [buttons](/terms/button) modeled after standard [scroll arrows](/terms/scroll-arrows) that are used to control a [scrolling list](/terms/scrolling-list) of text items within a specific [application](/terms/application).
