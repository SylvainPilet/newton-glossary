Title: Smirkers

----

Text:

The group of skeptical [Apple](http://apple.com/) employees working on the Newton project who doubted the feasibility and operability of both the larger [Newton Plus](/terms/newton-plus) and the [Dylan](/terms/dylan) programming language running on the device.
