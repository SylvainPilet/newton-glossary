Title: WALT

----

Text:

Wizzy Active Lifestyle Telephone

An experimental telecommunication device developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) and [BellSouth](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BellSouth) around the same time as the [Original MessagePad](/terms/original-messagepad). It featured a touchscreen [display](/terms/display) and [stylus](/terms/stylus), similar to the Newton, but with a HyperCard-based [user interface](/terms/user-interface) running on top of the Macintosh System 6.0.8 [operating system](/terms/operating-system).

The WALT has been referred to as “… a Mac in Newton’s clothing.”
