Title: Marco

----

Text:

A [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device developed by [Motorola](http://www.motorola.com/). The Marco featured a wireless modem that used the [Ardis](/terms/ardis) wireless networking service. The Macro is often referred to as a Newton [clone](/terms/clone).
