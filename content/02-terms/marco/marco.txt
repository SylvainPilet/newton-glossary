Title: Marco

----

Text:

A [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device developed by [Motorola](http://www.motorola.com/). The Marco featured a wireless modem that used the [Ardis](/terms/ardis) wireless networking service.

Third party devices that utilize a licensed version of the Newton OS are also referred to as [clones](/terms/clone).
