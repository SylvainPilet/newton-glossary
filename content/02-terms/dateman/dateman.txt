Title: DateMan

----

Text:

A Newton [application](/terms/application) created by [Standalone Software](http://standalone.com/) which works in conjunction with the built-in [Name File](/terms/name-file) and [Date Book](/terms/date-book) to give an overall view of your entire agenda. Also referred to as [DM](/terms/dm).
