Title: Info Button

----

Date: 06 December 2017

----

Text:

A small square [button](/terms/button) an “I” character, located in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) of an [application](/terms/application). [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the info button provides access to help and [preferences](/terms/preferences) related to the current application.

Also known as the [information button](/terms/information-button) in earlier versions of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).
