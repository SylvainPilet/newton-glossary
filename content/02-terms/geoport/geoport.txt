Title: GeoPort

----

Text:

A serial data standard developed by [Apple](http://apple.com/) and designed primarily for voice and telephony [applications](/terms/application), additionally supporting both [RS-232](/terms/rs232) and [RS-422](/terms/rs422) modes. The physical port itself is basically a standard 9-pin serial connector with the addition of a 5V DC pin that could facilitate the powering of an external device.
