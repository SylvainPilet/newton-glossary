Title: Keyboard Button

----

Date: 31 August 2017

----

Text:

A [button](/terms/button) that displays an onscreen keyboard for entering text and other types of information. When [tapped](/terms/tapped), the default [Typewriter](/terms/typewriter) keyboard appears. [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the button again while the keyboard is onscreen will display a [picker](/terms/picker) listing other types of keyboards, including [Numeric](/terms/numeric), [Phone](/terms/phone), and [Time/Date](/terms/time-date).
