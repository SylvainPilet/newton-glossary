Title: Original MessagePad

----

Text:

The very first commercially released Newton model. Not to be confused with the [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100) which shares the same model number ([H1000](/terms/h1000)). Commonly referred to as [OMP](/terms/omp).
