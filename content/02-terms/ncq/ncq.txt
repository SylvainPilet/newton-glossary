Title: NCQ

----

Text:

An instant messaging [application](/terms/application) for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). NCQ is compatible with the standard [ICQ protocol](https://web.archive.org/web/20140703194702/http://www.ihse.net/icq/), but only implements a subset of its features.
