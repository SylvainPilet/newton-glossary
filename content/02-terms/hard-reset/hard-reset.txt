Title: Hard Reset

----

Text:

A technique to completely erase all user data, including [applications](/terms/application) and [packages](/terms/package), from a Newton device. Also referred to as a [Brain Wipe](/terms/brain-wipe) or [Cold Boot](/terms/cold-boot).
