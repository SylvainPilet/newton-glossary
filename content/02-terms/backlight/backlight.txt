Title: Backlight

----

Text:

The [electroluminescent panel](/terms/electroluminescent-panel) which illuminates the [screen](/terms/screen) of certain Newton devices. The [eMate](/terms/emate-300), [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130), [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) are all outfitted with backlights.
