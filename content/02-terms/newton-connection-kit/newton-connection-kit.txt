Title: Newton Connection Kit

----

Text:

Software developed by Apple that allows users to exchange and synchronize information between a Newton device and common desktop applications. The Newton Connection Kit is for use with [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 1.x only. The equivalent software package for Newton OS 2.x is [Newton Connection Utilities](/terms/newton-connection-utilities). Also referred to as [NCK](/terms/nck).
