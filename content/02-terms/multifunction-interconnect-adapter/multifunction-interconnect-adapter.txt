Title: Multifunction Interconnect Adapter

----

Date: 04 November 2017

----

Text:

A prototype [dongle](/terms/dongle) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that provided standard connections for serial and [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) communication, audio input and output, and power when plugged into an [Newton Interconnect](/terms/newton-interconnect) port.
