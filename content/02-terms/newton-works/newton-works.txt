Title: Newton Works

----

Text:

An integrated software [application](/terms/application) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for devices running [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.1 only. Functionality includes a word processor, drawing tools, and a graphing calculator. On an [eMate](/terms/emate-300), Newton Works is the default [backdrop](/terms/backdrop) application.

Also referred to as [NewtWorks](/terms/newtworks) or simply [Works](/terms/works).
