Title: Ethernet

----

Text:

A family of packet-based networking technologies originally developed at [Xerox PARC](https://www.parc.com/) in the early 1970s. Ethernet connects computers and other devices over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network) (LAN), commonly using twisted pair cables with [RJ-45](/terms/rj-45) style connectors.
