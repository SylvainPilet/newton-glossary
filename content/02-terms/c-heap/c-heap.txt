Title: C++ Heap

----

Date:

----

Text:

An area of [system memory](/terms/system-memory) used by the [operating system](/terms/operating-system) specifically by native program [functions](/terms/function). Sometimes this part of the [heap](/terms/heap) is confused with all areas of system memory which are not part of the [NewtonScript heap](/terms/newtonscript-heap).
