Title: Newton Personal Data Sharing

----

Text:

A suite of software [packages](/terms/package) which allow certain Newton devices to act as web servers. Originally created by [Matt Vaughn](http://chromatin.cshl.edu/vaughn/) and subsequently developed by [Paul Guyot](/sources/pg), [Adam Tow](http://tow.com/), and [Victor Rehorst](/sources/vr).
