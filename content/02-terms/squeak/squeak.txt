Title: Squeak

----

Text:

The sound made by a Newton device after pressing and holding the [pen](/terms/pen) on the screen until a large dot known as a [heavy mark](/terms/heavy-mark) appears. Used to indicate the start of the action when [selecting](/terms/select) a block of text or a [shape](/terms/shape).
