Title: Power Reset

----

Text:

A method of resetting the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) by pressing the [reset button](/terms/reset-button) and holding down the power switch. A power reset does not erase any user data, applications, packages, [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) training, or preferences. However, it does reset the power manager. Also known as a [deep reset](/terms/deep-reset) or [hardware reset](/terms/hardware-reset).
