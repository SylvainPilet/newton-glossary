Title: Expansion

----

Date: 14 February 2018

----

Text:

A word or phrase that is automatically substituted in place of a user-defined abbreviation. Also known as an [autoexpansion](/terms/autoexpansion), an expansion is stored in the [expand dictionary](/terms/expand-dictionary].
