Title: Workfile

----

Text:

The main data file created by the [Newton Connection Kit](/terms/newton-connection-kit) software. The workfile contains all of the information used when [synchronizing](/terms/synchronize) a Newton device with a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop computer).
