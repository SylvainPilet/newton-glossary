Title: Newton Book

----

Text:

An electronic document for viewing on a Newton device, created using software such as [Newton Press](/terms/newton-press) or [Newton Book Maker](/terms/newton-book-maker), and delivered as an installable [package](/terms/package). Sometimes referred to as a [Newton Digital Book](/terms/newton-digital-book).
