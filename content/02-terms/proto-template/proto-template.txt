Title: Proto Template

----

Text:

A predefined [template](/terms/template) that defines the appearance and behavior of standard [user interface](/terms/user-interfaces) elements, such as a [close box](/terms/close-box) or [status slip](/status-slip). A proto template is often referred to as a [proto](/terms/proto) for short.
