Title: Newton Book Maker

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) for creating electronic documents in [Newton book](/terms/newton-book) format and online help for Newton [applications](/terms/application). Newton Book Maker uses text-based [book maker commands](/terms/book-maker-commands) contained in a [book source file](/terms/book-source-file) to produce a [book definition file](/terms/book-definition-file).

Bookmaker commands allow the developer to provide additional functionality or control over the page layout of a book. Book Maker also provides a means to attach other data, [methods](/terms/method), and view [templates](/terms/template) to book content.
