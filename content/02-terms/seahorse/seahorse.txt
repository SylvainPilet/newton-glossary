Title: Seahorse

----

Text:

A ruggedized [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) clone device developed by Digital Ocean. Similar to the Digital Ocean [Tarpon](/terms/seahorse), the Seahorse featured technologies such as the first [CDPD](/terms/cdpd) modem found in a [PDA](/terms/pda), built-in wireless networking, peer-to-peer voice communication, plus optional GPS and diffused [infrared](/terms/infrared) capabilities.
