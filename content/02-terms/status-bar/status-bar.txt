Title: Status Bar

----

Date: 25 November 2017

----

Text:

A thick black line with graphical [buttons](/terms/button) that perform functions related to the current [application](/terms/application).

Also known as a [button bar](/terms/button-bar) in later versions of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).
