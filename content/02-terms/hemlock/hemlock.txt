Title: Hemlock

----

Text:

An internet search tool for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [Sean Luke](/sources/sean-luke). Hemlock utilizes the [Newt’s Cape](/terms/newts-cape) web browser to retrieve and display the results of querying one or more search engines using Apple’s [Sherlock](http://web.archive.org/web/20011108064228/http://www.apple.com/sherlock/) plugins.

The name is in reference to the Sesame Street character, [Sherlock Hemlock](http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Sherlock_Hemlock).
