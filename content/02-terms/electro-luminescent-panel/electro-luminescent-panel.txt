Title: Electro-Luminescent Panel

----

Text:

An illumination technology consisting of a thin film phosphor layer sandwiched between a substrate. When charged, the phosphor in that emits visible light. The electro-luminescent [backlight](/terms/backlight) found in later Newton devices has a distinguishing bright green color. Also referred to as an [ELP](/terms/elp).
