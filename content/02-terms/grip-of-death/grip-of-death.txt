Title: Grip Of Death

----

Sort: Grip Of Death

----

Text:

A system freeze common in early [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) devices caused by a lack of adequate [heap space](/terms/heap-space). In later versions of the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) this condition notified the user of a problem instead of causing the operating system to become unresponsive.
