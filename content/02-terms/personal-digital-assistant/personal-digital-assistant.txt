Title: Personal Digital Assistant

----

Text:

A small handheld computer — such as a Newton device — that typically provides calendar, contact management, and note-taking applications. Small keyboards and pen-based input systems are commonly used for user input. The original term was coined by former Apple CEO [John Sculley](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Sculley). Commonly referred to as a [PDA](/terms/pda).
