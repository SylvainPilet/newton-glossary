Title: Touch Tone

----

Text:

The common term describing [Dual Tone Multi Frequency](/terms/dual-tone-multi-frequency) or [DTMF](/terms/dtmf) method of telephone tone dialing. Basically, the combining of two tones, one low frequency and one high frequency. The Newton can create Touch Tone signals using the telephone keyboard.
