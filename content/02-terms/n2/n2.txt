Title: N2

----

Text:

The code name of the hardware platform built on the [Voyager](/terms/voyager) chip set and the base platform for [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices. N2 hardware could theoretically support up to four [PCMCIA](/terms/pcmcia) slots, 4MB of [DRAM](/terms/dram), 12MB of [Flash RAM](/terms/flash-ram), and 16MB of [ROM](/terms/rom). It was also developed for third-party licensed or [clone](/terms/clone) Newton devices.
