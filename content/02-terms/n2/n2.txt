Title: N2

----

Sort: N2

----

Text:

The code name of the hardware platform built on the [Voyager](/terms/voyager) chip set and the base platform for [MP2x00](/terms/mp2x00) series of Newtons. N2 hardware could theoretically support up to four PCMCIA slots, 4MB of DRAM, 12MB of Flash RAM, and 16MB of ROM. It was also developed for third-party licensed or [clone](/terms/clone) Newton devices.
