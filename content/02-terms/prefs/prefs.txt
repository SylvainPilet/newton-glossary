Title: Prefs

----

Date: 03 September 2017

----

Text:

The built-in [application](/terms/application) for setting various options or [preferences](/terms/preferences) pertaining to the operation and fine-tuning of a Newton device. [Tapping](/terms/tapping) a topic in the Prefs list will open a [slip](/terms/slip) where setting may be entered or updated.

_This entry requires a screenshot._
