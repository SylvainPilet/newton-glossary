Title: Newtourage

----

Text:

A desktop [application](/terms/application) created by [John Del Pizzo](http://johndelpizzo.wordpress.com/) that allows a Newton device to synchronize contact information between the [Names](/terms/names) application and [Microsoft Entourage](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Entourage).
