Title: MessagePad 2100

----

Text:

The last Newton device released by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/). The MessagePad 2100 shares the same specifications as its predecessor, the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-130), with the exception of the amount of [RAM](/terms/ram). A MessagePad 2000 can been upgraded to the equivalent of a MessagePad 2100 by increasing the RAM from 5MB to 8MB.
