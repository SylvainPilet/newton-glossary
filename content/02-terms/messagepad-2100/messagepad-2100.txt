Title: MessagePad 2100

----

Text:

The last Newton device released by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) through their [Newton Inc.](/terms/newton-inc) subsidiary. The MessagePad 2100 shares the same specifications as its predecessor, the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), with the exception of the amount of [RAM](/terms/ram). A MessagePad 2000 can been upgraded to the equivalent of a MessagePad 2100 by increasing the RAM from 5MB to 8MB.

Like its predecessor, the MessagePad 2000, the MessagePad 2100 was code named [Q](/terms/q). It is commonly referred to as an [MP2100](/terms/mp2100).
