Title: King of Newtonia

----

Text:

A nickname for the otherwise practical Rich Lindsay, proprietor of the [This Old Newt](/terms/this-old-newt) web site. The title of king was proclaimed by acclamation during the 2001 [Newtie Awards](/terms/newtie-award).
