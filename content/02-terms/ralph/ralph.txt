Title: Ralph

----

Sort: Ralph

----

Text:

The code name for [Dylan](/terms/dylan), the dynamic programming language developed at Apple that was to run on the original Newton platform. Named for [Ralph Ellison](http://www.biography.com/people/ralph-ellison-9286702), the author of _The Invisible Man_. Also referred to as [Denali](/terms/denali).
