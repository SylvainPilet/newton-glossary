Title: Ralph

----

Text:

The code name for [Dylan](/terms/dylan), the dynamic programming language developed at Apple that was to run on the original Newton platform. Named for Ralph Ellison, the author of The Invisible Man. Also referred to as [Denali](/terms/denali).
