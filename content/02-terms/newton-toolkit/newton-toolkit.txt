Title: Newton Toolkit

----

Text:

A development environment created by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) for writing [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) programs for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). Also referred to as [NTK](/terms/ntk).

<h2>Additional Information</h2>

[UNNA: Newton Toolkit](http://www.unna.org/view.php?/apple/development/NTK)
[The Newted Community: Newton Toolkit Enhancements](http://www.newted.org/download/manuals/NewtonToolkitEnhancements164.pdf) (PDF)
