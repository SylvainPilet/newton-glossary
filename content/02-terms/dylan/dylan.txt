Title: Dylan

----

Text:

The high-level dynamic programming language developed at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that was to run on the original Newton platform. Known for being slow and difficult to write programs with. Also referred to as [Denali](/terms/denali) and [Ralph](/terms/ralph).
