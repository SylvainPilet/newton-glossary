Title: Heavy Mark

----

Text:

A large dot that appears when you hold the [pen](/terms/pen) down in one place on the Newton screen. Used to start the action of [selecting](/terms/select) a block of text or a [shape](/terms/shape). A [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device will audibly [squeak](/terms/squeak) when the dot appears.
