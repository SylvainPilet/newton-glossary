Title: Mont Blanc

----

Text:

The code name of the final [Newton Plus](/terms/newton-plus) product design. This device was apparently engineered and tooled for production before being shelved and subsequently replaced by the [Pocket Newton](/terms/pocket-newton), code named [Junior](/terms/junior).
