Title: Rosetta

----

Text:

Code name for the software-based [print recognizer](print-recognizer) system developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/). Rosetta first shipped in the second revision of the [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120) device. The name was eventually changed to [Mondello](/terms/mondello) for legal reasons. Although never used publicly, the official product name for the print recognizer was [Neuropen](/terms/neuropen).
