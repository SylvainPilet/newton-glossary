Title: System Patch Pages

----

Text:

Memory pages that replace the [ROM](/terms/rom) or [ROM Extension](/terms/rom-extension) pages of the [patchable jumptable](/terms/patchable-jumptable) or the [magic pointers table](/terms/magic-pointers-table).
