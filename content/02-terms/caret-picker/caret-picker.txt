Title: Caret Picker

----

Date: 05 December 2017

----

Text:

A [picker](/terms/picker) that appears when the user [taps](/terms/tap) the [caret](/terms/caret). The caret picker allows a user to easily choose a punctuation mark, space, paragraph break, or other special character to be input at the text [insertion point](/terms/insertion-point) indicated by the caret.
