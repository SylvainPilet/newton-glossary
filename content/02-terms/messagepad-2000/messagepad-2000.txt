Title: MessagePad 2000

----

Text:

The MessagePad 2000 is based on the StrongARM 110 processor, making it nearly ten times as fast as its predecessor, the [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130). It also featured significantly more [RAM](/terms/ram), a slightly larger greyscale [display](/terms/display), support for two [PCMCIA](/terms/pcmcia) cards, and the proprietary multifunction [Newton Interconnect](/terms/newton-interconnect) port.

When an MessagePad 2000 has been upgraded to the equivalent specifications of a MessagePad 2100, it becomes an [UMP](/terms/ump) or “Upgraded MessagePad”. A non-upgraded MessagePad 2000 is often referred to as an [Original MessagePad 2000](/terms/original-messagepad-2000) or [OMP2K](/terms/omp2k).
