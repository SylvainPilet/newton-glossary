Title: Proto

----

Text:

A [frame](/terms/frame) referenced through another frame’s `_proto` [slot](/terms/slot). With regard to [views](/terms/view), a proto is not intended to be directly [instantiated](/terms/instantiate) — you [reference](/terms/reference) the proto from a [template](/terms/template). The system supplies several view protos (or [proto templates](/terms/proto-template)), which an [application](/terms/application) can use to implement [user interface](/terms/user-interfaces) elements such as [buttons](/terms/button) and [input areas](/terms/input-areas).
