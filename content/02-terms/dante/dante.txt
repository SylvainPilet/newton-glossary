Title: Dante

----

Text:

The code name of the [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130) device. The MessagePad 130 has also been referred to by the code name [Quark](/terms/messagepad-130).

Dante also refers to version 2.0 of the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), as well as the connection [protocol](/terms/protocol) used to communicate between a Newton device and another computer.
