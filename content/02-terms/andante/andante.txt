Title: Andante

----

Text:

The code name for the Newton device that was to be next in line after the [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100). According to [Walter R. Smith](/sources/walter-smith), the development priorities of this device were “placed on size and price”. Andante never shipped due to Newton development being [Steved](/terms/steved).
