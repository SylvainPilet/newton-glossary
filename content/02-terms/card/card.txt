Title: Card

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) of information pertaining to an entry in the [Name File](/terms/name-file) application [soup](/terms/soup), graphically formatted as a business card.

Also, an [application](/terms/application) that allows the user to retrieve information about a PC card inserted into a Newton device. If the PC card is an [external store](/terms/external-store), the Card application can be used to rename, backup, restore, and erase information stored on the card.

Card can also refer to the [PC Card](/terms/pc-card) itself.
