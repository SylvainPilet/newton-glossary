Title: Card

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [view](/terms/view) of information pertaining to an entry in the [Name File](/terms/name-file) application [soup](/terms/soup), graphically formatted as a business card.

Card is also an [application](/terms/application) that allows the user to retrieve information about a PC card inserted into a Newton device. If the PC card is an [external store](/terms/external-store), the Card application can be used to rename, [backup](/terms/backup), [restore](/terms/restore), and erase information stored on the card.

Card can also refer to a [PC card](/terms/pc-card) or [CompactFlash](/terms/compactflash) card.
