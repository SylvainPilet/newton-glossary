Title: Hard Button

----

Text:

A permanent hardware [button](/terms/button) displaying an [icon](/terms/icon) found in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) below the screen of early Newton devices, including the [OMP](/terms/omp), [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100), [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110), [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), and [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130). A hard button is used either to open a built-in [application](/terms/application) such as [Dates](/terms/dates) or to perform a system-wide operation such as undo.
