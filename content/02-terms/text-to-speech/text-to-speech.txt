Title: Text-to-Speech

----

Text:

The conversion of text into audio output through some means of speech synthesis. Performing text-to-speech on a Newton device requires the [MacInTalk](/terms/macintalk) speech synthesizer [extension](/terms/extension) and the [SpeechText](/terms/speechtext) transport. Also known as [TTS](/terms/tts).
