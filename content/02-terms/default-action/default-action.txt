Title: Default Action

----

Sort: Default Action

----

Text:

The completion action that users are most likely to take among the safe alternatives in the [key view](/terms/key-view). For example, [tapping](/terms/tapping) the “Ok” [button](/terms/button) or pressing the “Enter” key when presented with a [confirmation slip](/terms/confirmation-slip).
