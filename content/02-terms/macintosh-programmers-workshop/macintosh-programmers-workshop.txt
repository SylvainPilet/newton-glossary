Title: Macintosh Programmers Workshop

----

Sort: Macintosh Programmers Workshop

----

Text:

A comprehensive collection of core development tools from Apple designed to support C, C++, and assembly language programmers developing Macintosh software. Also referred to as [MPW](/terms/mpw).

Macintosh Programmers Workshop is required by the [Newton C++ Toolbox](/terms/newton-c-toolbox) when creating C++ code for the Newton.
