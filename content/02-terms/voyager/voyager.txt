Title: Voyager

----

Text:

The name of the [ASIC](/terms/asic) chipset developed by [Cirrus Logic](http://www.cirrus.com/) and used in the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices. Officially known as the [CL-PS7000](/terms/cl-ps7000) chipset, Voyager consisted of four components including a [CPU](/terms/cpu) subsystem controller, an analogue controller, a [PCMCIA](/terms/pcmcia) controller, and an [infrared](/terms/infrared) transceiver.

Voyager was sometimes referred to by the [Cirrus](/terms/cirrus) code name.
