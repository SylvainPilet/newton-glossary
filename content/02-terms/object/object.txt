Title: Object

----

Text:

A typed piece of data that can be an [immediate](/terms/immediate) value, [array](/terms/array), [frame](/terms/frame), or [binary object](/terms/binary-object). In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), only frame objects can hold [methods](/terms/method) and receive [messages](/terms/message).
