Title: HyperNewt

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) developed by [Adam Tow](/sources/adam-tow) that extends the built-in [folder](/terms/folder) filing system of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). HyperNewt provides flexible data management controls, hierarchical organization, and hyperlinking of items from most applications, including [Notepad](/terms/notepad), [Date Book](/terms/date-book), [Calls](/terms/calls), [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer), [InOut Box](/terms/inout-box), and [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works).
