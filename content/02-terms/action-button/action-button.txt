Title: Action Button

----

Text:

A button used in [applications](/terms/application) to invoke [routing](/terms/routing) functions. When [tapped](/terms/tapped), it displays a [picker](/terms/picker) listing routing actions available for the current item, otherwise known as an [action picker](/terms/action-picker). Also referred to as the [envelope button](/terms/envelope-button) or [routing button](/terms/routing-button).
