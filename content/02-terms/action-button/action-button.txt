Title: Action Button

----

Text:

The small [envelope button](/terms/envelope-button) used in [applications](/terms/application) to invoke [routing](/terms/routing) functions. When [tapped](/terms/tap), it displays a [picker](/terms/picker) listing routing actions available for the current item. Also referred to as the [routing button](/terms/routing-button).
