Title: Egg Freckles

----

Text:

An easter egg found in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.0 which when activated displays an image from a Doonesbury cartoon lampooning the accuracy of the early Newton [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition). Egg Freckles is also the name of Newton-themed weblog written by [Thomas Brand](http://eggfreckles.net/).

cf. http://myapplenewton.blogspot.ca/2011/08/dilbert-compares-smartphones.html
