Title: Egg Freckles

----

Text:

An easter egg found in Newton OS 2.0 which when activated displays an image from a Doonesbury cartoon lampooning the accuracy of the early Newton handwriting recognition. [Egg Freckles](http://eggfreckles.net/) is also the name of Newton-themed weblog written by Thomas Brand.

http://myapplenewton.blogspot.ca/2011/08/dilbert-compares-smartphones.html
