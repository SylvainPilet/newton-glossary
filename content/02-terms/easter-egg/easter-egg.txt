Title: Easter Egg

----

Text:

A message, image, inside joke, or feature that has been intentional hidden in an [application](/terms/application), [operating system](/terms/operating-system), or other media. Easter eggs are generally activated by a series of undocumented commands or actions.

Examples of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) easter eggs include [Area 51](/terms/area-51) and [Egg Freckles](/terms/egg-freckles).
