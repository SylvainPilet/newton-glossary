Title: Hot Spot

----

Text:

A small unnamed and often unmarked [control](/terms/control) that responds like a [button](/terms/button) when [tapped](/terms/tap). Usually there are several hot spots in a given [view](/terms.view), which may be visible or invisible to the user.
