Title: Caret

----

Text:

A [cursor](/terms/cursor) symbol (^) that indicates where the Newton will insert text written, printed, or typed by the user. He caret symbol is only used in the 2.x versions of the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os). Earlier versions of Newton OS used a [blinking line](/terms/blinking-line) cursor to indicate where new text would be inserted. A caret shaped [gesture](/terms/gesture) can be used to when editing text to indicate where a new [insertion point](/terms/insertion-point) should be placed.
