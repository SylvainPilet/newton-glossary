Title: Stationery

----

Date:

----

Text:

The capability of having different kinds of data within a single [application](/terms/application). For example, the ability to display text [notes](/terms/note), checklists, and outlines in the [Notepad](/terms/notepad) application. Stationery also facilitates the viewing of the same data in different ways — such as the “Card” and “All Info” views in the [Name File](/terms/name-file) application.

Implementing stationery involves writing [data definitions](/terms/data-definition) and [view definitions](/terms/view-definition).
