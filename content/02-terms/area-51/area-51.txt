Title: Area 51

----

Text:

An [easter egg](/terms/easter-egg) found in the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) which when activated displays the [icon](/terms/icon) for a meeting as a person facing an alien, the icon for an event as a flying saucer, to-do tasks are represented by a robot, and so on.
