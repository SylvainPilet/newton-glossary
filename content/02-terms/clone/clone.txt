Title: Clone

----

Text:

A device manufactured by a third party company using Newton technology licensed from [Apple](http://apple.com/). Newton clones included devices such as the Sharp [ExpertPad](/terms/expertpad), Motorola [Marco](/terms/marco), Digital Ocean [Tarpon](/terms/tarpon), Harris [Access Device](/terms/access-device), Harris Dracon [SuperTech](/terms/supertech-2000), Schlumberger [Watson](/terms/watson), and Siemens [NotePhone](/terms/notephone).
