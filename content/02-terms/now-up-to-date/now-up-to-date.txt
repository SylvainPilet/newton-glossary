Title: Now Up To Date

----

Text:

A desktop calendaring and scheduling [application](/terms/application) developed by [Now Software](https://sites.google.com/site/nowsoftwarecom/), some versions of which can synchronize information with a Newton device. Also referred to as [NUD](/terms/nud).
