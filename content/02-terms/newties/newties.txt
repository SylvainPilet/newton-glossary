Title: Newties

----

Text:

An affectionate nickname for Newton users.

[The Newties](https://web.archive.org/web/20050205024243/http://www.thisoldnewt.net/html/newties.html) were also an annual award presented to various members of the Newton community by Rich Lindsay of [This Old Newt](/terms/this-old-newt) fame.
