Title: Backdrop

----

Text:

An [application](/terms/application) that is displayed when all other applications are closed and cannot be closed itself. [Notepad](/terms/notepad) is the default backdrop application on a [MessagePad](/terms/messagepad) device. [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works) is the default backdrop application on an [eMate](/terms/emate-300). Another application can set as the backdrop by using the [action picker](/terms/action-picker) located in the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer).
