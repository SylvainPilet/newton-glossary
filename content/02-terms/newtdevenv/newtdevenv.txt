Title: NewtDevEnv

----

Sort: NewtDevEnv

Text:

A development environment created by [Steve Weyer](http://communicrossings.com/) for creating [applications](/terms/application) using [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) directly on a Newton device. Also referred to as [NDE](/terms/nde) and [Newt](/terms/newt).
