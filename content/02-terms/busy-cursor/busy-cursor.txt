Title: Busy Cursor

----

Date:

----

Text:

A graphical indicator automatically displayed by the system while it is temporarily unable to process user [input](/terms/input). Often represented by an [icon](/terms/icon) of a watch or clock, the busy cursor in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) is represented by an depiction of the Newton logo.

Colloquially known as the [lightblub icon](/terms/lightblub-icon) or the [somethingâ€™s happening icon](/terms/somethings-happening-icon).
