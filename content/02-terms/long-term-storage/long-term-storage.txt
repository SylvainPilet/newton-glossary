Title: Long Term Storage

----

Text:

An extended period of disuse of a Newton device or other piece of equipment. Part of the preparation process prior to long term storage is to remove the main and backup batteries. This helps prevent damage to the Newton device due to battery leakage. Also referred to as [LTS](/terms/lts).
