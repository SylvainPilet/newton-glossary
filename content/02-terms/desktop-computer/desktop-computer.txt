Title: Desktop Computer

----

Text:

A computer system that generally resides on a desk or table, as opposed to a portable laptop or tablet computer. In terms of a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) environment, the desktop computer would likely be running either the Mac OS or Windows operating system.
