Title: Slow Infrared

----

Text:

Transferring data using an [IrDA](/terms/irda)-compatible [infrared](/terms/infrared) connection at a rate up to 115 kBaud. As opposed to [Fast Infrared](/terms/fast-infrared) which can transfer data at a rate up to 4 MBaud. The [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) utilize Slow Infrared or [SIR](/terms/sir) transceivers.
