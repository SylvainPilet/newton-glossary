Title: Unfiled

----

Date: 26 October 2017

----

Text:

A [folder](/terms/folder) containing items which have not been filed in another folder. In the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer), this folder is called [Unfiled Icons](/terms/unfiled-icons).
