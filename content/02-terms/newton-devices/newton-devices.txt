Title: Newton Devices

----

Text:

A [package](/terms/package) containing various driver functions allowing communication using [protocols](/terms/protocol) such as [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) and [TCP/IP](/terms/tcp-ip). The Newton Devices package incorporates the [Lantern Library](/terms/lantern-library) of driver functions, and other driver APIs ([application programming interfaces](/terms/application-programming-interface)).
