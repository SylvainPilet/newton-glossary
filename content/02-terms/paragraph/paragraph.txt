Title: ParaGraph

----

Date: 07 September 2017

----

Text:

Another name for the cursive (or connected) recognizer according to the [Catamount Software](/sources/catamount) handwriting tips page. Likely in reference to [ParaGraph International](/sources/paragraph), the company who created the [CalliGrapher](/terms/calligrapher) handwriting recognition engine.
