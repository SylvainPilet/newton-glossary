Title: StrongARM

----

Text:

The low-power [RISC](/terms/risc) processor used in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) devices, developed by [ARM](/terms/arm) in conjunction with [Digital Equipment Corporation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Equipment_Corporation).
