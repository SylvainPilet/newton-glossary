Title: StrongARM

----

Text:

The low-power [RISC](/terms/risc) processor used in Newton devices, developed by [ARM](/terms/arm) in conjunction with [Digital Equipment Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Equipment_Corporation).
