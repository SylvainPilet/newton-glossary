Title: AppleLink

----

Date: 19 March 2017

----

Text:

An online service used by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) between 1986 and 1994 to support and provide information to employees, dealers, developers, and end users. Newton devices could exchange messages with [AppleLink](/terms/applelink) using the [NewtonMail](/terms/newtonmail) service. AppleLink was eventually superseded by the short lived [eWorld](/terms/eworld) service in 1994.
