Title: Nickel Cadmium

----

Date:

----

Text:

A rechargeable [battery](/terms/battery) technology that is susceptible to [memory effect](/terms/memory-effect), unlike batteries based on [Nickel Metal Hydride](/terms/nickel-metal-hydride) technology. Also referred to as a [Nicad](/terms/nicad) or [NiCd](/terms/nicd) battery.
