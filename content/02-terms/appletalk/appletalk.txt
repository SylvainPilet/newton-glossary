Title: AppleTalk

----

Text:

A routable networking [protocol](/terms/protocol) developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) for communications between Mac OS computers and other platforms over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network). AppleTalk supports networking layers such as [LocalTalk](/terms/localtalk) and [Ethernet](/terms/ethernet).
