Title: Developer Technical Services

----

Text:

A group within [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) which provided technical support and programming tools for third party software and hardware developers. Commonly referred to as [DTS](/terms/dts). The current group is now called [Developer Technical Support](https://developer.apple.com/support/technical/submit/).
