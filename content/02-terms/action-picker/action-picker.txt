Title: Action Picker

----

Text:

A listing of various [routing](/terms/routing) actions available for the current item. Activated by [tapping](/terms/tapping) on the [action button](/terms/action-button). The action button has the [icon](/terms/icon) of an envelope on it and is sometimes referred to as the [envelope button](/terms/envelope-button) or [routing button](/terms/routing-button).
