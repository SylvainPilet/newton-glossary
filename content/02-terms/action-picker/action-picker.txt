Title: Action Picker

----

Sort: Action Picker

----

Text:

A listing of various [routing](/terms/routing) actions available for the current item. Activated by [tapping](/terms/tapping) on the [Action Button](/terms/action-button). The Action button has the icon of an envelope on it and is sometimes referred to as the [Envelope Button](/terms/envelope-button) or [Routing Button](/terms/routing-button).
