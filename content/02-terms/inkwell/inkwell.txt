Title: InkWell

----

Text:

The purported name of the Mac OS version of the Newton [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system, eventually known simply as the [Ink](/terms/ink) preference pane in early releases of Mac OS X. The InkWell interface consisted of the [InkBar](/terms/inkbar) toolbar and the [InkPad](/terms/inkpad) notepad.
