Title: Newton Messaging Card

----

Text:

A [PC card](/terms/pc-card) developed by [Motorola](http://www.motorola.com/) that allowed a Newton device to receive and store wireless network messages over a variety of paging systems and news services. The Newton Messaging Card appears to have been based on the Motorola [NewsCard](/terms/newscard), which shares similar specifications.
