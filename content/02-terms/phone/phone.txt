Title: Phone

----

Date: 31 August 2017

----

Text:

An onscreen keyboard that appears when the [keyboard button](/terms/keyboard-button) is [tapped](/terms/tapped). The phone keyboard is designed for easily entering telephone numbers into [applications](/terms/application) such as [Name File](/terms/name-file). Several other onscreen keyboards are available for entering specific types of data, including [Typewriter](/terms/typewriter), [Numeric](/terms/numeric), and [Time/Date](/terms/time-date).

_This entry requires a screenshot._
