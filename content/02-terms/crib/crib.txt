Title: Crib

----

Sort: Crib

----

Text:

Code name of the charging cradle designed to work with the [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110), [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), and [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130) devices. Also referred to as the [Lindy Crib](/terms/lindy-crib), after the code name for the MessagePad 110.
