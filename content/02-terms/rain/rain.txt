Title: RAIN

----

Text:

Redundant Array of Inexpensive Newtons

Coined by Ed Kummel.

http://marc.info/?l=newtontalk&m=101293531830713&w=2

The term is a play RAID, the acronym for “Redundant Array of Individual Disks”.

https://groups.google.com/forum/?hl=en#!msg/comp.sys.newton.misc/szCYICTUOLE/xEbxdVo9vJkJ

“Newton Farm” — Sean Luke

Also, any collection of Newton devices where multiple instances of the same model exist.
