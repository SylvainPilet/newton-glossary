Title: In Box

----

Date:

----

Text:

A list in the [InOut Box](/terms/inout-box) application where received items can be viewed, filed, [routed](/terms/routing), and otherwise managed. The In Box and [Out Box](/terms/out-box) are separate [applications](/terms/application) in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 1.x.
