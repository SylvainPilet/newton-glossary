Title: Hammer

----

Sort: Hammer

----

Text:

A low level debugger for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) development that runs on a Macintosh. Used to debug C, C++, or [ARM](/terms/arm) assembler code that will eventually run on a Newton device.
