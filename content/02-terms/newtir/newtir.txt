Title: NewtIR

----

Text:

An [infrared](/terms/infrared) communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) based on the [Amplitude Shift Keying](/terms/ask) (ASK) standard and used in older Newton devices. This [protocol](/terms/protocol) includes extensions developed by [Apple](http://apple.com/) which make it substantially faster.
