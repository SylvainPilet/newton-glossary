Title: NewtIR

----

Text:

An [infrared](/terms/infrared) communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) based on the [Amplitude Shift Keying](/terms/amplitude-shift-keying) (ASK) standard and used in older Newton devices. This protocol includes extensions developed by Apple which make it substantially faster.
