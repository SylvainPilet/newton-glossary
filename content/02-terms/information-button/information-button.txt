Title: Information Button

----

Date: 19 April 2017

----

Text:

A [button](/terms/button) in the shape of a small square box with an [I](/terms/i) inside located in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) of an [application](/terms/application). [Tapping](/terms/tapping) the information button provides access to help and [preferences](/terms/preferences) related to the current application.
