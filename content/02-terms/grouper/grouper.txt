Title: Grouper

----

Date: 17 July 2017

----

Text:

A range of radio-based devices developed by [Digital Ocean](https://web.archive.org/web/19970124001013/http://digitalocean.com/) that provide [wireless AppleTalk](/terms/wireless-appletalk) connectivity to [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) and Macintosh computers over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network).
