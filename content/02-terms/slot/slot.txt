Title: Slot

----

Text:

An element of a [frame](/terms/frame) or [array](/terms/array) that can hold an [immediate](/terms/immediate) value or [reference](/terms/reference) value.

A slot is also an opening where a [PC Card](/terms/pc-card) is inserted into a Newton device.
