Title: Dongle

----

Text:

An adapter that plugs into the proprietary [Newton Interconnect](/terms/newton-interconnect) port found on the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate](/terms/emate-300) devices, converting it to a standard [Mini-Din](/terms/mini-din) serial connector.
