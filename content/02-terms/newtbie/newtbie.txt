Title: Newtbie

----

Text:

A brand spanking new member of the Newton community. Newtbie is a play on “newbie”, the slang term used to describe someone who is a novice or newcomer, especially relating to gaming or the internet. If all goes well, a Newtbie eventually grows up to be a [Newtie](/terms/newtie).
