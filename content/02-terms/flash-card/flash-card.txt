Title: Flash Card

----

Text:

A memory card using [flash RAM](/terms/flash-ram) chips that can be rewritten and hold its content without power. Commonly referred to as a [storage card](/terms/storage-card). Not to be confused with a [CompactFlash](/terms/compactflash) (CF) card, which is similar in function but different in size and implementation.
