Title: Transport

----

Text:

An [object](/terms/object) in [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) that provides a communication service to the [In/Out Box](/terms/in-out-box) application, interfacing with an [endpoint](/terms/endpoint). Examples include the print, fax, [beam](/terms/beam), and mail transports.
