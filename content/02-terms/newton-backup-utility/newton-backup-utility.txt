Title: Newton Backup Utility

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](http://apple.com/) that allows you to backup and [restore](/terms/restore) data from a Newton device to a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer). Also referred to as [NBU](/terms/nbu).
