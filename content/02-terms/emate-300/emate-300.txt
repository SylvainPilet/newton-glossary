Title: eMate 300

----

Text:

A translucent, clamshell-cased Newton device with a built-in keyboard. Primarily aimed at the educational market. Also known by various code names such as [K](/terms/k), [Schoolbook](/terms/schoolbook), and [Shay](/terms/shay).
