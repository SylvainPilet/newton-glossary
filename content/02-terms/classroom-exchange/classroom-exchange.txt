Title: Classroom Exchange

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) that facilitates the connection of one or more [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices to a Mac OS computer and the simultaneous transferring of information between those devices.

_This entry requires a screenshot._
