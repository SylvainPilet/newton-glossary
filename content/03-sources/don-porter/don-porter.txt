Title: Don Porter

----

Text:

Don Porter was a mechanical engineer with the [Newton Systems Group](/terms/newton-systems-group) and worked on the development of the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000). He is also the person responsible for selecting the connector used for the infamous [Newton Interconnect](/terms/newton-interconnect) port.
