Title: NewtonTalk Mailing List

----

Text:

Founded by Sean O’Shaughnessy in 1997, the [NewtonTalk](/terms/newtontalk) mailing list provides an open forum dedicated to the discussion, support, and enjoyment of Apple’s Newton platform and related technologies.

NewtonTalk is currently maintained by [Grant Hutchinson](/sources/grant-hutchinson).
