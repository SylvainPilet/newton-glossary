Title: The Apple Online Museum

----

Text:

The _Apple Online Museum_ was founded in 1998 by Lukas Foljanty and original created as a “result of reading Owen Linzmayer’s _The Mac Bathroom Reader_”. The concept of the site was to to combine the history of Apple products with specifications and other related background information. The site was also known as simply _The Apple Museum_ in later iterations.
