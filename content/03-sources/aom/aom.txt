Title: The Apple Online Museum

----

Text:

The Apple Online Museum was founded in 1998 by Lukas Foljanty. Started as “a result of reading Owen Linzmayer’s _The Mac Bathroom Reader_”, the idea of the site was to to combine the history of Apple products with their specifications and other related information. The site was also known as _The Apple Museum_ is later iterations.
