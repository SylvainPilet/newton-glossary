Title: Catamount Software

----

Text:

A software development company formed by Hardy Macia in 1994. Catamount specializes in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), Palm OS, and iOS [applications](/terms/application), including [Aloha](/terms/aloha), [Silk](/terms/silk), and PocketMoney.
