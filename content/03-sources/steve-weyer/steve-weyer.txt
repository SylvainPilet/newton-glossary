Title: Steve Weyer

----

Text:

A self-proclaimed technology addict, Steve spent two decades in high profile organizations such as Stanford University, Xerox PARC, Atari, Hewlett Packard, and Apple before becoming an independant consultant and [software developer](http://communicrossings.com/html/newton/). Steve has created an incredible amount of software for the Newton platform, including the [Newt’s Cape](/terms/newts-cape) web browser, the [NewtDevEnv](/terms/newtdevenv) development environment, and the [Sloup](/terms/sloup) data transfer utility.
