Title: Portable Design

----

Text:

A trade magazine focused on providing engineering solutions and solving problems unique to the design of portable products, including the [Personal Digital Assistant](/terms/personal-digital-assistant). Originally published by [PennWell Corporation](http://www.pennwell.com/) between 1995 and 2007, _Portable Design_ was eventually acquired by [RTC Group](http://www.rtcgroup.com/), finally shuttering the publication in 2009.
