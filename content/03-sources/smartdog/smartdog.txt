Title: Smart Dog Software

----

Date:

----

Text:

A company founded by Robert Ellison that developed [BackTalk](/terms/backtalk), software which providing modern infrared communication protocols for the Newton platform. Smart Dog Software also released several games for the Newton, including a variation of _Settlers of Catan_.
