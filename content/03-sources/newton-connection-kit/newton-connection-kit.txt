Title: Newton Connection Kit User’s Guide

----

Text:

The [Newton Connection Kit](/terms/newton-connection-kit) is a consumer product developed by [Apple](http://www.apple.com/) for connecting Newton devices running [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 1.x to Macintosh and Windows computers.
